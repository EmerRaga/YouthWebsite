Meet the Team

All of our work that we've done and will do would not be possible without the help and support of our amazing team, whether it's front line event organizing to BTS website development and video editing, we thank each and every single one of them.


We are proud to come from different parts!

Our team represents 4 countires across 3 continents, expanding our reach to all corners of the globe!

Australia

Uyghur Youth International is based in Australia, and is our home of operations. All international efforts are held there.

Canada

Canada is our base of operations for North America. Our team there coordinates in-person events held across North America.
